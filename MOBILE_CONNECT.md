# How to Install Fitness Tracker on Your Phone

To get the "One Click" app experience on your phone, follow these steps just once.

## Step 1: Start the App on Computer
1.  Double-click **`start_app.bat`** on your computer.
2.  Wait for the terminal window to show something like:
    ```
    Network: http://192.168.1.15:5173/
    ```
    *(The number `192.168...` is your computer's local IP address)*.

## Step 2: Open on Phone
1.  Ensure your phone is connected to the **same Wi-Fi** as your computer.
2.  Open your phone's web browser (Chrome or Safari).
3.  Type the Network address exactly as shown in the terminal (e.g., `192.168.1.15:5173`).

## Step 3: Add to Home Screen (The "One Click" Trick)
Once the app loads on your phone:

### iPhone (Safari):
1.  Tap the **Share** button (box with an arrow up) at the bottom.
2.  Scroll down and tap **Add to Home Screen**.
3.  Tap **Add**.

### Android (Chrome):
1.  Tap the **Menu** button (three dots) at the top right.
2.  Tap **Add to Home screen** (or "Install App").
3.  Tap **Add**.

**Success!** You now have a "Fitness Tracker" app icon on your home screen.
Tapping it will open the app immediately in full-screen mode, just like a real app.
